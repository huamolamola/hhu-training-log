Progressive Overload
Add 1–2 reps or 1 kg every 1–2 weeks. Small consistent increases lead to big results over months.
🎯
Felt Score Guide
1 = extremely hard · 2 = hard · 3 = moderate · 4 = easy · 5 = very easy. If you score 5 twice in a row, increase the weight.
⏱️
Rest Between Sets
Rest 30–45 seconds between sets to stay within 30 minutes. Rest longer (60–90s) if your form starts to break down.
🩹
Form First
Slow down on the way down (2–3 seconds). This eccentric phase is where most muscle building happens.
💤
Rest Days Matter
Muscles grow on rest days, not during workouts. Keep Tue, Thu, Sat, Sun as rest. Light walking is fine.
📊
Monthly Review
Check your Progress tab every 4 weeks. Seeing real numbers go up is the best motivation to keep going.
🏋️
Band Placement
Band above knees = glute activation during squats. Band at ankles = isolation for donkey kicks and fire hydrants.
🥩
Protein Matters
Aim for ~1.6g of protein per kg of bodyweight daily to support muscle building. Eggs, chicken, tofu, and legumes all count.
💧
Stay Hydrated
Drink water before, during, and after each session. Even mild dehydration reduces strength output by up to 10%.
